The Leeds LGBTQ+ Hack

A Leeds Digital Festival:
20 September - 1 October 2021

Register Your Team

The Coders Guild are running a Hack as part of Leeds Digital Festival.

We're calling on anyone to register a team to create an idea - it could be an app or a website, it could be a piece of music (or even a cake!) anything which will make Leeds more inclusive for the Leeds LGBTQ+ community.

Key Hack Dates:

20 Sept: Leeds LGBTQ+ Hack Launch event (6-8pm) at ODI Leeds. Registered teams will be briefed on the rules, format and deadline for submissions.


20-28 Sept: Leeds LGBTQ+ Hack - Teams get hacking!


28 Sept: Deadline for hack submissions.


1 Oct: Leeds LGBTQ+ Hack Awards (5pm-8pm) at Duke Studios.
